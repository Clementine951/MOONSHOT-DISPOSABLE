About Disposable

Our Story

Disposable was born from a simple idea: to simplify event photo sharing in a temporary, secure way. I, Clémentine Curel, noticed that managing permanent event photos was cumbersome and intrusive. Disposable allows users to capture and share moments instantly while ensuring that the photos remain ephemeral.

What began as a personal project during my computer science studies has grown into a platform designed to empower people to create and join events effortlessly, sharing memories without the clutter of permanent storage.

Vision: To create elegant, intuitive tools that enhance the way we capture and share memories while respecting privacy.

Our Mission & Vision

Our mission is to empower users to capture and share their most cherished moments quickly and securely. Disposable combines simplicity with advanced privacy features, ensuring that your memories are shared in the moment and then fade away, keeping your digital space uncluttered.

Looking ahead, we aim to continuously enhance the app with new features, improved security, and a seamless user experience, all while staying true to our commitment to ephemeral, privacy-conscious sharing.

Future Plans

Disposable is a work in progress, and many exciting features are on the horizon. We plan to introduce enhanced sharing options, advanced privacy controls, and more intuitive event management tools. Your feedback is invaluable, and we look forward to evolving Disposable together.
